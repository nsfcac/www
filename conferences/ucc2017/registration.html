Registration

The conference is located in the events area of the AT&T Conference Center, just across the central courtyard from the main lobby of the hotel. Check-in will be available during the following hours:

  • Monday: 4:00 - 6:00 pm
  • Tuesday: 7:30 am - 6:00 pm
  • Wednesday: 7:30 am - 5:00 pm
  • Thursday: 7:30 am - noon
  • Friday: 7:30 am - noon

The registration desk will be on the lobby level (first floor) Monday and Tuesday, and will move to the second floor for the rest of the conference. Contact info@ucc-conference.org for more information.

Registration is available now at the following link.

*** Register now! ***

Registration rates depend on your membership status as an ACM or IEEE member and include a student discount if applicable. Rates change from early to regular values after October 5, and from regular to late/onsite values after November 5.

ACM or IEEE members: Early $725 (Student $500), Regular $800 (Student $575), Late $875 (Student $650)
General - non-members: Early $825 (Student $600), Regular $900 (Student $675), Late $975 (Student $750)
One-day pass: Early $300, Regular $350, Late $400

Don't forget also to reserve your lodging at the special conference rate of $189 plus tax per night
to stay at the conference venue! (Rate available for check-in on or after December 4 and check-out on or before December 9 through this link. For stays beyond this time period, contact the hotel directly.)

Further information on registration and on reasons to stay at the conference hotel.
